Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Anchor: ADHD ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — email, name, and username.
  • Profile data — ADHD type, goals, and country.
  • Check-in data — mood, energy, and motivation.
  • App usage — lessons, routines, and streaks.
  • Community content — posts and comments.
  • Device information — basic technical data about the device you use.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve the app.
  • Generate personalized AI insights and coaching.
  • Power leaderboard and community features.
  • Send push notifications.
  • Process subscription payments via RevenueCat.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. AI Features

Our AI coach and pattern insights are powered by the Anthropic Claude API. Conversation data is used only to generate responses and is not used to train AI models.

5. Subscriptions

Payments are processed by Apple. We do not store your payment information.

6. Your Rights

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen. When you do, all of your data is permanently deleted.

7. Children's Privacy

Anchor: ADHD is intended for users 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hello@anchoradhd.app.
